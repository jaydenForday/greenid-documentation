# Admin Panel

The main greenID Administration Panel screen is displayed when you log in. On this screen, you can:

1. Access the greenID Business screens or

2. Look up the personal verification records of individuals.

The following actions can be performed on the Business Verification [Admin Panel](https://vixverify.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/GREEN/pages/491520911/Admin+Panel) screen:

- [Procedure 1. Access greenID Business](https://vixverify.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/GREEN/pages/491520911/Admin+Panel#AdminPanel-Procedure1.AccessgreenIDBusiness)

- [Procedure 2. Find a personal verification record for an individual](https://vixverify.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/GREEN/pages/491520911/Admin+Panel#AdminPanel-Procedure2.Findapersonalverificationrecordforanindividual)

## Procedure 1. Access greenID Business

On the [Admin Panel](https://vixverify.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/GREEN/pages/491520911/Admin+Panel) screen:

1. Choose **Business Verification** from the Admin Panel menu.

*Figure 1. GreenID Administration Panel*

![](/img/bus-admin-panel.webp)

The [Manage Business Verifications](https://vixverify.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/GREEN/pages/492470563/Manage+Business+Verifications) screen is displayed.

You can return to the Admin Panel screen from within Business Verification by selecting the **Back to the main greenID Admin Panel** button, which appears at the top of any greenID Business screen:

![](/img/bus-verification.webp)

## Procedure 2. Find a personal verification record for an individual

On the [Admin Panel](https://vixverify.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/GREEN/pages/491520911/Admin+Panel) screen:

1. Enter your search criteria in one or more of the fields shown under Search or filter for customers.

2. Select **Search**.

(For more information on what you can do in greenID Admin panel refer to [Admin Panel Guide](https://vixverify.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/GREEN/pages/14286881/Admin+Panel+Guide).)
