# Appendix A - Business Registers

The following table shows the types of business numbers that may be entered in greenID’s [Verify a Business Wizard](https://vixverify.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/GREEN/pages/492306827/Creating+a+verification+record#Creatingaverificationrecord-figure3), the registries that verify them, and the types of information that may be downloaded. The types of business numbers vary by country.

*Table 3: Business Numbers (By Country)*

| **Country** | **Business Number**                                                                                                                  | **Business Types**                                                       | **Business Register**              | **Data Retrieved**                                                                                                                               |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Australia   | ACN (Australian Company Number)                                                                                                      | Companies (registered with ASIC)                                         | ASIC (via Creditorwatch)           | <p>Company office holders</p><p>Shareholders (except if a listed company)</p>                                                                    |
|             | ARSN (Australian Registered Scheme Number)                                                                                           | Managed Investment Schemes (registered with ASIC)                        | ASIC (via Creditorwatch)           | <p>Controllers (eg. Responsible Entity)</p><p>Other Parties of Interest (eg. Auditors)</p>                                                       |
|             | ARBN (Australian Registered Body Number)                                                                                             | Registered Australian Bodies                                             | ASIC (via Creditorwatch)           | Conrollers (eg. Directors)                                                                                                                       |
|             | ABN (Australian Business Number)                                                                                                     | <p>Partnerships</p><p>Sole Traders</p><p>Trusts</p><p>\[Companies\*]</p> | Australian Business Register (ABR) | Only the existence of the business on the register is verified. No additional data is retrieved.                                                 |
| New Zealand | NZBN (New Zealand Business Number) or NCO (New Zealand Company Number) or other organisation number recognised by the NZBN register  | <p>Companies</p><p>Partnerships</p><p>Sole Traders</p><p>Trusts</p>      | NZBN Register                      | <p>For Companies: Directors, Shareholders</p><p>For Partnerships\*: Partners</p><p>For Trusts\*: Trustees</p><p>For Sole Traders: Proprietor</p> |
