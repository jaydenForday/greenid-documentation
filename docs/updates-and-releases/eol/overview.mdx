# Overview

greenID consistently reviews its products, services and performance to ensure we are providing the best identity verification solution in market.  At times we make decisions to remove or end-of-life a service or product.  In these instances we will always endeavor to provide adequate notification to impacted customers.  This page is designed to provide further detail on some of the services we are discontinuing.
